FAQ

Is blockchain secure?

Blockchain is considered secure due to its cryptographic features and decentralized nature. However, the security of a blockchain depends on the implementation, and vulnerabilities can still exist.

Can anyone read a smart contract?

Yes, smart contracts on public blockchains are transparent, and anyone can access and review the code. This transparency contributes to trust but can also expose vulnerabilities.

Can NFT ownership be transferred?

Yes, NFT ownership can be transferred between wallets. This is typically done through the marketplace or platform where the NFT was created or purchased.

What happens after a smart contract audit?

After an audit, the auditor provides a report outlining findings, recommendations, and suggested fixes. Developers then address the issues found and retest the contract.
